## Overview
Link="https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/online+retail"
Online retail is a transnational data set
 which contains all the transactions occurring 
between 01/12/2010 and 09/12/2011 for a UK-based and registered non-store online retail.
 The company mainly sells unique all-occasion gifts. Many customers of the company are wholesalers.

## Business Goal
We aim to segement the Customers based on RFM so that the company can target its customers efficiently.



note:
Use KMeans, Agglomerative Clustering , DBScan and Validate the clusters 
output from each algorithm by analytics
